How to play the game:
w is move up
s is move down
a is move left
d is move right

press space to interact with object or select an option from any of the menus
press tab in the world map to open inventory
press escape to go back to the previous menu or in the world map the escape button opens the pause menu

the white tiles is the shop press space to enter the shop
the red tiles are to progress get to the last one and beat the boss to beat the game
